Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Will it get too hot in summer?

Our summer houses are designed with ventilation and glazing options to keep the space comfortable, even on warmer days.

Can I add insulation later?

Yes — insulation can be added as an optional extra at the time of order, or retrofitted afterwards.

Do you install throughout the year?

Yes, we install year-round, though good weather makes for the smoothest installation.

What maintenance is required?

An annual check of the timber treatment and guttering will keep your summer house looking its best for years to come.
