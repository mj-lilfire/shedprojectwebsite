Working From Home

A Professional Workspace, Right at Home

A proper garden office means a real separation between work and home life — without the commute. Fully insulated for year-round use, our garden offices are built with contemporary architecture and quality materials, so they feel like a genuine professional environment, not a shed with a desk in it.

Large windows keep the space bright and connected to the garden, while solid construction keeps it quiet, warm, and ready to work in whatever the weather.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Will I get a good internet connection?

Most customers use a wireless extender or run an ethernet cable during installation — we can advise on the best approach for your garden.

Is it insulated for winter working?

Yes, every garden office is fully insulated as standard, with electric heating available as an optional extra.

Do I need building regulations approval?

Most garden offices don't require building regulations approval, though this depends on size and intended use — we'll guide you through what applies.

Can I fit it out with my own furniture?

Of course — we deliver a finished shell ready for you to fit out exactly as you like.
