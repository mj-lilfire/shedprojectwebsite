About Us

A Family Business Built on Craftsmanship

Hereford Patio & Sheds is a family-run business dedicated to designing and building premium outdoor spaces. From practical storage to fully insulated garden rooms, every building we create is finished to the same exacting standard — because a garden building should feel like part of your home, not an afterthought.

Every project is built using quality timber and premium materials, then installed by our own experienced team — so you get a beautiful, long-lasting space with professional installation and no compromises.
